Websocket

A communication protocol that enables full-duplex, bidirectional communication channels over a single TCP connection. It allows real-time data exchange between a client and server, making it ideal for applications like chat apps, live notifications, and online gaming, where low latency and immediate interaction are crucial. WebSockets maintain an open connection, reducing the overhead of traditional HTTP requests.
